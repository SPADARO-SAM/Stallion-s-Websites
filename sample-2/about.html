Our Story

Over 25 years of building trust, structures, and communities — one project at a time.

Built on Integrity Since 1998

Sample Construction Co. was founded on a simple belief: that great buildings start with great people. Since opening our doors in 1998, we've grown from a small residential contractor into one of the region's most trusted full-service construction firms.

Today, our team of 50+ skilled professionals delivers commercial, residential, and civil projects across the state — always on time, always on budget, always with the craftsmanship our clients deserve.

We don't just build structures. We build relationships that last long after the final nail is driven.

Our Journey

Key milestones in over two decades of building excellence.

1998

Founded in Anytown

Sample Construction Co. opens its doors with a team of five, focusing on residential remodels and small commercial projects.

2004

First Major Commercial Contract

Awarded a $4.2M contract for the Downtown Commerce Center — our entry into large-scale commercial construction.

2011

Regional Builders Award

Recognized by the Regional Builders Association for outstanding quality and safety on the Creekside Bridge project.

2018

50-Person Team Milestone

Expanded to a full-service team of 50+ specialists, adding a dedicated design-build division and project management office.

2026

400+ Projects Completed

Celebrating over 400 successful project completions and 98% client satisfaction across residential, commercial, and civil sectors.

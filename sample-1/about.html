Our Story

Sample Construction Co. was founded in 1998 by brothers James and Robert Carter with a single work truck, a small crew, and a commitment to doing things right. What started as a local residential contractor has grown into one of the region's most respected full-service construction companies.

Over the past 25+ years we've delivered more than 400 projects — from custom homes and commercial office parks to civil infrastructure and large-scale renovations. Through every phase of growth, our core promise has stayed the same: quality work, honest pricing, and a job site you can be proud of.

Today we employ over 50 skilled tradespeople, project managers, and support staff — all sharing a passion for building things that last.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every decision we make on and off the job site.

Safety First

Every worker goes home safe. We maintain rigorous safety protocols on every job site, no exceptions.

Integrity

We say what we mean and mean what we say. Honest estimates, honest timelines, honest communication.

Craftsmanship

We take pride in every nail, beam, and finish. If it's not right, we make it right — at our expense.

Community

We live and work here too. We source locally when possible and invest in the communities we build in.

Innovation

We adopt new materials, methods, and technology to deliver better results for clients and the environment.

Partnership

Our clients are partners, not transactions. We succeed when you succeed — and we treat every project that way.
